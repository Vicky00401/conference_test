  ABOUT AICASETM'24

ACE International Conference on Applied Science, Engineering, Technology and Management 2024 (AICASETM ‘ 24) call out for solutions for the booming problems and issues in the field of Engineering,Science and Management. This conference is aimed at providing a platform for academicians, scientists, R&D institutions, industrial experts, research scholars, undergraduate and post graduate students to showcase their work. It will be a Forum to share and exchange various innovations, ideas, applications, and endeavours in the field of Engineering ,Science and Management.
